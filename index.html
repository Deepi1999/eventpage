Life of Ram - Tamil Standup Comedy
Ramkumar is a seasoned standup comedian, humorist, public speaker and a recent Evam YouTube sensation from Chennai. He grew up in Tirunelveli, as a son of a strict Maths teacher, but acquainted with all the mollamaarithanams in the world. He is a jack of many many trades that includes fitness, half and full(only marathons), cycling, yoga, traveling, blogging, public speaking, pranking people effectively and what not. On the other side, he also works as a Product Manager and has a more than 16 years of IT experience with 6 different companies in 3 different cities. He, in fact, changed his company once because there was no good Andhra mess near his office. He is also married, has a 7-year-old, and lives in a community with 350 other families. Is there a need to say where he gets his jokes from..! Please book, come and hear some family friendly jokes.
